Welcome to the Mangrove Mapping System

Harnessing cutting-edge technology to accurately map and visualize the distribution and health of mangrove forests, aiding in effective conservation and management strategies. Let us provide you with some important information:

  • Mangroves are vital coastal ecosystems, providing habitat for diverse marine life and protecting coastlines. At the Mangrove Mapping System, our mission is to utilize drone technology for accurate mapping and monitoring, enabling informed decision-making and promoting the sustainable management of mangrove ecosystems.
  • Conservation Awareness and Education: The visual outputs generated by the mapping system can be used to raise public awareness about the importance of mangrove ecosystems. Engaging visualizations and maps facilitate educational outreach, fostering a greater understanding and appreciation for mangroves among communities, stakeholders, and the general public.
